On Depression and Recovery

January 17, 2019

Depression is a very heavy handed word, and probably the most misunderstood one too. It stealthily engulfs you in its tight grasp, attackiong when you least expect it to. One moment you’re enjoying the weather, basking in the glory of some happy thought, maybe remembering that on thing that your partner did for you - and suddenly from this wave of happy and positive emotions, a tiny splash comes towards you - the splash of doubt. Was your colleague being sarcastic when he was praising your work?
